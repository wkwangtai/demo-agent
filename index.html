Why I Started NEXTSIX

Property advertising should not feel like a race to upload the most listings.

It should be about helping the right buyer discover the right property through the right agent.

That is the problem I have been obsessed with solving. NEXTSIX started with a simple belief: when advertising creates genuine value for property seekers, everyone wins — buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants, and agents.

Today, we are building technology, content, and advertising solutions designed to make property discovery more transparent, more engaging, and more effective.

The Mission Is Bigger Than Listings

Most people see a property portal. I see an opportunity.

An opportunity to improve how properties are presented, how agents build trust, and how consumers make one of the biggest decisions of their lives.

Every feature we launch is guided by one question: Will this help someone make a better property decision?

If the answer is yes, we build it.

Move The Market. Don't Let It Move You.

Better advertising does not just grow businesses. It helps people find homes.
